Poplar Farm, Clevedon, Somerset BS21 6UN

Travel

Car: Poplar Farm is just 10 minutes from Junction 20 M5. Please be weary of cyclists and pedestrians along the country roads near the Farm.

Parking: There is plenty of parking available at Poplar Farm but please note that this is not immediately next to camping and bell tent areas. There will be clear signage when you arrive.

Train: The closest train station is Yatton. Either pre-book a taxi or ask a friend to collect you â€“ it is about a 10 minute drive.

Cycle: If you are feeling adventurous, Poplar Farm is along National Cycle Route 33. North Somerset has some beautiful countryside to explore along the cycle routes.

Taxis: We would advise to book a taxi or Uber in advance as they are not frequent in the area. Local Companies: North Somerset Taxis, Streets Ahead Taxis, Triangle Cars Clevedon
