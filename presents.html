Presents

As we prepare to say “I do,”
We’re filled with joy,
and thanks to you,
Our friends and family, near and dear,
Who’ve cheered us on year after year.

We’re building a home with love and care,
With cozy cushions, plates to spare.
So rather than a gift-wrapped box,
Or kitchen tools or pairs of socks...

We’d treasure something just as sweet:
A little help to make ends meet.
A honeymoon, a future bright,
You’d help us launch it just right.

No pressure, though, it’s not a must,
Your presence is the gift we trust.
But if you’d like to bless our day,
A small donation goes a long way.

With love and laughter, wine and song,
We’ll dance together all night long.
And think of you with grateful hearts,
For playing one of the biggest parts.

Whelnett Wedfest Ltd.
