WHAT TO BRING

  • Train tickets and railcard
  • ID
  • Money/cards
  • Mobile phone and battery pack
  • Ear plugs
  • Water bottle
  • Wellies or sturdy boots
  • Waterproof coat and over trousers
  • Umbrella
  • Change of weather appropriate clothing (in case you get wet!)
  • Warm clothing as it can get chilly at night
  • Toiletries including towel/soap
  • Medication if required
  • Medical info (allergies etc)
  • Sun cream and hat
  • Torch/batteries/bulb
  • Bin bags (for dirty clothing and footwear)
  • Mark valuables with name so it can be posted back to you
  • Drinks
